The Restaurant

In addition to an unforgettable golf experience, stop by our clubhouse restaurant and take in the 180 degree panoramic views of the golf course whilst dining on a selection of local and international delicacies.
If the air conditioned indoors is not for you then why not try The Terrace, an outdoor seated area a short pitch away from the 18th green where you can relax in comfort whilst watching your playing partners finish their round.
Alternatively if a more secluded feel is what you seek, then our private dining rooms will be just right for you. Two spacious rooms that can seat up to 25 people with additional casual seating and private terrace. Why not enjoy your own private BBQ in the comfort of your own space surrounded
Food & Beverage is also available in abundance on the golf course. Our kiosks on hole 4 & 15 are the best in town with air conditioning and even free Wi-Fi available, it is the perfect place to cool off and recharge for a few minutes whilst enjoying a snack and refreshing beverage.
