Mecure Ba Na Hills French Village
There are few places in Vietnam that offer such a rich combination of nature, comfort and beauty. The Mercure Ba Na Hills French Village is a unique architectural work of ancient French villages: square, church, town, villages, hotels on the top of a mountain 1,500 metres above sea level.

Perfect for groups and situated on the picturesque My An Beach, take time to relax and enjoy the proximity to the ancient town of Hoi An, the Cham ruins at My Son and the beautiful Ba Na Hills.
Our ultra-modern villas are your perfect home away home, complete with a private pool and secluded garden. Large bedrooms and spacious en-suite bathrooms give our guests a sense of space.
